About Me

Hi, I`m Liz. I`m a Full Stack Developer and UX Designer. I served in the military for 6 years and loved every second of it, but I needed something new that would challenge me. I was introduced to coding by a SGT of mine and that is when my journey began. So I started to teach myself some code. I began with vanilla HTML and CSS and instantly fell in love. I wanted to learn more but didn`t know where to start. That`s when I learned about bootcamps. I enrolled in devCodeCamp, it was a 12 week Full Stack course. I graduated December 2022. But I wasn`t done, I wanted to learn more! So I enrolled in Google`s course for UX Design. I learned a lot of amazing things including how to make the User Experience better and more inclusive. As I continue my journey I have started taking Scrimbas course for Front End Development and plan to learn a lot more. I am excited to start a career and learn from more experienced developers and also share my knowledge in the process. If you have any questions please reach out, I would love to hear from you.
